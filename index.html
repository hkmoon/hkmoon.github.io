About

Parallelism in computer systems is not easy to understand because we have been caught in sequential thinking. My work focuses on breaking these barriers — designing algorithms that harness GPU and grid computing to process terabytes of microscopy data, building open-source scientific tools used by researchers worldwide, and bridging the gap between cutting-edge biological research and production-grade software engineering. With 25+ publications, 1,000+ citations, and contributions to the ImageJ/Fiji ecosystem, I turn complex scientific problems into elegant, performant software.
